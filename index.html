About me

I completed a Master of Science in Applied Mathematics at the University of Science, Vietnam National University, HCMC under the supervison of Assoc. PhD. Vo Si Trong Long on the topic of existence solutions of continuous optimization problems.

Previously, I completed a Master's Degree in Computer Science at the same institution. I was privileged to work under the guidance of Prof. PhD. Le Hoai Bac on the topic of Temporal Knowledge Graph Reasoning Based on Reinforcement Learning.

Prior to my graduate studies, I earned a Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science, where I conducted research on Knowledge Graph Completion Based on Convolutional Neural Networks under the supervision of Dr. Le Ngoc Thanh.

My interested research is about mathematical programming and numerical methodsalgorithms for optimization. I like to read about ODE PDE books and research papers.
