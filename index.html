About me

I am currently pursuing a Master of Science in Applied Mathematics at the University of Science, Vietnam National University, HCMC under the supervison of Assoc. PhD. Vo Si Trong Long.

Previously, I completed a Master's Degree in Computer Science at the same institution. I was privileged to work under the guidance of Prof. PhD. Le Hoai Bac on the topic of Temporal Knowledge Graph Reasoning Based on Reinforcement Learning.

Prior to my graduate studies, I earned a Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science, where I conducted research on Knowledge Graph Completion Based on Convolutional Neural Networks under the supervision of Dr. Le Ngoc Thanh.

My primary research focus is on graph mining, particularly in the area of knowledge graphs, including challenges related to completion and reasoning. In addition to this, I possess a deep interest in theoretical computer science—covering areas such as graph theory and its applications—as well as in mathematics, where my passions lie in mathematical programming, numerical methods, and topics related to probability.

Research Interests

  • Reinforcement Learning

  • Graph Mining
